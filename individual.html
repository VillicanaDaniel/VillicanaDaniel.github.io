ButlerHell

ButlerHell was a project done at SMU Guildhall as a personal exploration into RPG systems and to play a joke on my professor. ButlerHell mimicks the battle style and HUD of Undertale in order to deliver a fun and humorous RPG bullet hell experience.

Link to Github Repo

Doomenstein: GOLD

Doomenstein: GOLD was a project made at SMU Guildhall as an additional to one of the curriculum's projects. Doomenstein: Gold borrows systems from the original DOOM and Wolfenstein titles in order to create a short boss rush game. The player must dodge bullet-hell like patterns in this first person shooter to progress and reach the end of the game.
