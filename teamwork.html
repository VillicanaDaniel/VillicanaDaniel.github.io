Nekomoto

Role: Programmer

Nekomoto is an action platformer game where players take control of a cat ronin cutting through multitudes of mafia dog enemies. Players slash, dash, and deflect their way through increasingly difficult levels.

Hamsterballin'

Role: Game Designer

Hamsterballin' is a local multiplayer arcade kart racing game where players control hamster balls through unique toy-themed environments. The game features unique physics based gameplay where players bounce and ramp off the surrounding play area to succeed. Other kart racing features such as Grand Prix, Single Race, Items, and costumes are also present.
